Find Your Dream Internship

With InternQuest, you can easily discover and apply to the best internships in your field. Our platform makes it simple to find opportunities that match your interests, skills, and career goals. Start your journey today and take control of your career!

I am a student I am a company

InternQuest is an online platform that connects students with internship opportunities. With a wide range of internships available in various industries and fields, students can easily find and apply to opportunities that align with their interests and career goals.

Features for Students

  • Browse and apply to a wide range of internships
  • Track your applications and receive updates on your status
  • Connect with mentors and other students in your field

Features for Organizations

  • Create and manage internship opportunities
  • Review and select the best candidates for your internship
  • Connect with students and other mentors in your field
